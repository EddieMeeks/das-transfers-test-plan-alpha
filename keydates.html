Key Dates

What’s coming up in the transfers calendar?

The next few months

End of April

● You’ll be able to see your transfers allowance in your apprenticeship service account.

● A new transfers estimator tool will help you plan the apprenticeships you can fund with a transfer.

● There’ll be more support videos online to help employers register on, and use, the apprenticeship service, including how to make and receive transfers.

May

● Employers that do not pay the levy will be able to register for an apprenticeship service account which will enable them to receive a transfer.

● You’ll be able to agree to fund apprenticeships in one other organisation through a transfer.

● Receiving employers will be able to start adding details of their apprenticeships to the service.

June

● The first payments will be paid for apprenticeship starts added in May. The apprenticeships will need to be approved by the sending employer before payments are made.

If you have employers in mind that you’re thinking of working with, please share this information with them to help them get prepared for transfers.
