Introduction to Transfers

We want to make the transfer scheme as simple as possible. That’s why we’ve created this information hub, your one-stop-shop for all things transfers. Here you’ll find the latest updates and how tos to help you make the most of your apprenticeship levy, created especially for levy-paying employers.

How transfers benefit your business

Whether upskilling your supply chain, developing skills within your sector or supporting a local enterprise, transfers give you more say in how you spend your apprenticeship contributions.
