Benefits

You may wish to transfer funds to another business within your sector to help your industry retain skills and promote the learning of new ones.

In this way, you can best equip your industry for its future needs.


You can transfer funds to any business that’s smaller, larger or equal size to your own, provided they are not transferring funds themselves.

How your business can benefit from sending a transfer

The transfer funding scheme has been designed to give you more control to invest your apprenticeship contributions in a way that directly benefits your business.


Whether that’s supporting the upskilling of a smaller business in your supply chain or partnering with another levy-paying employer to create a workforce that’s skilled to the specific needs of their sector, transfers give businesses more flexibility in how they contribute to the future economy.

Upskill your supply chain for a better product for your business

You can invest in the training of your supply chain to improve specific skills that will ensure a better quality of product and help your business grow by meeting your organisation’s needs.


97% of apprentices said their ability to do the job had improved thanks to their on-the-job training.

Support a local business or charity as part of your CSR


Supporting an apprentice is an ideal corporate social responsibility enterprise. As well as inspiring young people into a new career path and creating opportunities to gain new skills and a recognised qualification whilst earning money, your funding transfer can give businesses who otherwise wouldn’t be able to take on an apprentice the chance to do so, supporting their growth and boosting the country’s skilled workforce.
